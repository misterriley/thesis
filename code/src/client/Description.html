Hello, and thank you for taking part in this study.

You will be able to complete this study using only the tab and arrow keys to navigate, and the enter/return key to indicate a selection.

This study comprises the following elements:

Informed consent page. Please read this page before consenting and save a copy for your records.

Two questions about gaming habits.

Three questions about demographics.

An eight question personality inventory.

Three tasks (Kinesthetic, Visual, and Verbal) presented in random order. Each will have a short tutorial, a practice session where you may adjust the difficulty to your liking, and a test session with 15 trials at a fixed difficulty level.

Debriefing document. Please read this page and save a copy for your records.

Comments and data submission.

Estimated time to completion: 10 - 15 minutes
